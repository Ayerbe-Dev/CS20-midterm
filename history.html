Green Mountain Outfitters

Our Story


Our founder, Henry Forest, was a lover of the outdoors from a young age. As a child he would often venture off into the woods behind his house and as he grew older, would spend hours exploring local trails and hiking mountains. He had a deep appreciation for the natural world and was a lover of adventure.

As he grew into a teenager and eventually a young adult, Henry became accustomed to taking on longer and more dangerous journeys he often had to fend for himself. He and his fellow adventures often had to make do with drinking from rivers, sleeping in shoddy tents with no heat and traveling either by foot or with vehicles ill suited to rough terrain.

These experiences gave Henry an idea. What if there was a company that could provide tools and equipment for the adventures of the world. A company, that at the same time could spread his love of the outdoors with the wider population. And so, Green Mountain Outfitters was born.

Although our founder has unfortunately passed way, Green Mountain and Henry's dream of helping spread the spirit of adventure and aiding those who pursue it still lives on. Once a small enterprise, Green Mountain has become one of the largest companies in its field will continue to pursue Henry's dream.
